History

Founded in 1995, Rapids Adventures began as a small, family-owned business with a passion for the great outdoors and a love for the exhilarating sport of white water rafting. Our journey started on the pristine rivers of Colorado, where our founders, John and Sarah Rivers, saw an opportunity to share their adventurous spirit with others. In our early years, we operated with just a handful of rafts and a dedicated team of friends and family. Despite our modest beginnings, our commitment to safety, adventure, and customer satisfaction quickly earned us a loyal following. Word of mouth spread, and Rapids Adventures began to grow, attracting thrill-seekers from all over the country. By the early 2000s, our reputation had solidified, and we expanded our operations to include multi-day expeditions, family-friendly trips, and specialized tours that catered to both novice rafters and seasoned adventurers.
